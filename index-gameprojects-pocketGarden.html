Pocket Garden

Portable 2D Garden Management Game

1 People | Windows (7, 10), Webgl, Android | Unity Engine | 6 Months



Project Info:

A simple game of garden management that you can pass your time away.
Manage your garden with tools provided to you, and remember to sold off your plant to get some money.
To pay off your debt every 300 seconds :]

My roles in this project as Project Manager and Game Developer.
Design the feel of garden management game, simulate plant growing when time passed.
